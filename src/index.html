Purpose

The diet cooper project lists all data sources within the GHDx that are tagged with any of the following keywords:

  • Diet
  • Dietary fiber
  • Dietary sodium
  • Fruits
  • Vegetables
  • Fish
  • Caloric intake
  • Processed meats
  • Unprocessed red meat
  • Nuts
  • Milk
  • Dairy products
  • Legumes
  • Sugar-sweetened beverages
  • Whole grains
  • Food expenditures
  • Household expenditures
  • 24HR dietary recall

Since survey microdata is given extraction priority, this flow chart breaks down the Cooper project by data type, and then further by whether or not microdata is present, and then finally by the different categories of diet microdata.

This flowchart also highlights potential areas for data extraction in the future, such as comperehensive tabulations and report tabulations.
